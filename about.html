About Me

Originally from Salina, KS, I hold a Bachelors Degree in Music from Fort Hays State University. After graduation, I landed my first job as a drummer for Royal Caribbean Cruise line where I played the main stage headlined shows while cruising the beautiful Pacific Northwest and Hawaii!

An ambitious, upbeat Full-Stack Web Developer with excellent presentation skills. Graduate of the University of Kansas with a Full-Stack Coding certificate with a focus on MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, Node.js). Experience partnering with teams to apply frameworks like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js to conceptualize and design apps such as RX|FM and CaptureMe.

Versatile background in technical training, well versed at delivering presentations and giving instruction in a formal classroom setting. Capable of bringing Frontend and Backend value to create a more robust and effective mobile app and website.
