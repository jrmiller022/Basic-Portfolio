About Me

I am originally from Salina, KS. I have a bachelors degree in music from Fort Hays University where I studied percussion. After I graduated I got my first job as a drummer for Royal Caribbean Cruise line where I played the main stage headlined shows while cruising the beautiful Pacific Northwest and Hawaii!
I am very ambitious and eager in learning and enjoying technology. From the first time I saw code it really grabbed me in and I wanted to figure out everything I could about it. Knowing that technology is an everyday use for me, I really want to know how it works and how I can expand on it. I enjoy learning new languages like React.js and how it’s different from pure javascript. And how the server side works with storing data within all the languages. I’m always looking forward to what’s new and coming out in the future.

I have a certificate from the University of Kansas as a Full-Stack Web Developer. With a focus on MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, Node.js).
