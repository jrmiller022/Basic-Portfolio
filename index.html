About Me

I am originally from Salina, KS. I have a bachelors degree in music from Fort Hays University where I studied percussion. After I graduated I got my first job as a drummer for Royal Caribbean Cruise line where I played the main stage headlined shows while cruising the beautiful Pacific Northwest and Hawaii!
After a short tour I moved to Kansas city where I have been for 11 years. I worked as a Musical theatre drummer at local theatres like the Rep, Amercian Heartland, New Theatre and Startlight theatre.
After my 5 yrs in musical theatre production I moved onto a job with a major cable company where I started out as an installer and worked my way up to now technical trainer. My hobby is 4-Wheelin' with Jeep Wranglers and belong to a Jeep club. I'm currently in coding bootcamp where I am very excited and egar to learn a new profession.

After completing KU Fullstack Bootcamp, I have experience with Front End and Back End with a focus on MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, Node).
